Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision: A thriving ocean ecosystem supporting biodiversity and human wellbeing worldwide.

Mission: To protect, restore, and sustain ocean environments through community engagement, scientific research, and education.

Core Values:

  • Integrity: Transparent, honest actions in all we do.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with communities and experts.
  • Sustainability: Long-term solutions for ocean health.
  • Respect: Honoring the ocean and all its inhabitants.
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas and technologies.

The Story Behind “Oceanowe”

Founded in 2020, Oceanowe began as a small group of ocean enthusiasts driven by a shared passion to protect our planet’s blue heart. Inspired by the powerful waves and fragile coral reefs, the name “Oceanowe” combines “ocean” with “owe” — a reminder that we owe the ocean our care and respect. From grassroots cleanups to reef restoration projects, Oceanowe has grown into a global community dedicated to empowering people and preserving prosperity for future generations.
